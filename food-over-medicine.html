Client Website Case Study

Food Over Medicine 🌿

A wellness-focused content platform designed for a nutrition educator to support trust, readability, and future course growth.

Project Summary

Timeline

2 Weeks

2025

Role

UX/UI Design

Site Structure & Styling

Deliverables

  • Responsive Blog Layout
  • Brand Styling
  • Ghost CMS Setup

Design Focus

Calm and Trustworthy Experience

The visual language was designed to feel calm, credible, and welcoming, supporting trust for readers exploring health content and future services.

Flexible Content Growth

The structure supports future growth through articles, recipes, and courses, allowing the site owner to publish content independently.

Key Design Decisions

Multilingual Content Structure

The website was designed to support both English and Bulgarian content. A simple tag-based content system allowed articles to be filtered using language tags such as #en and #bg, helping the client manage bilingual publishing clearly.

Flexible Client Content Management

The client wanted full control over adding recipes, courses, and articles independently, so the structure was planned to support clear categories, future growth, and easy self-management.

Live Website

Designed to create a calm reading experience with clear structure, strong readability, and room for future course-based content.

Design Approach

The design focused on calm spacing, clean hierarchy, and simple navigation so visitors can easily engage with educational content and build trust in the brand.
