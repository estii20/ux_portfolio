My Story

With a strong foundation in both creative problem-solving and technical execution, I'm a freelance UX/UI designer and junior full-stack web developer. My background in event management sharpened my communication and collaboration skills — essentials I now bring into my work designing digital products that prioritise usability, accessibility, and business impact.

I recently earned a Diploma in Fullstack Software Development, gaining hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, and responsive design frameworks like Tailwind CSS. I specialize in crafting mobile-first, user-centered interfaces and building scalable front-end components that align with both user needs and stakeholder goals.

Whether collaborating with cross-functional teams or independently delivering pixel-perfect UI designs in Figma, I approach every project with attention to detail, empathy for users, and a drive for excellence. I’m now open to freelance or junior roles in UX/UI design, front-end development, or hybrid positions where design meets code.
