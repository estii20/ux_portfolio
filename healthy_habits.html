Healthy Habits Coach App Design

A personal mobile application design project aimed at helping adults develop and maintain healthy lifestyle habits through intuitive UX/UI.

UX Research UI Design Mobile App Health Tech

Project Overview

Challenge

Design a user-friendly app that helps busy adults develop healthy habits through personalised coaching and progress tracking.

Solution

Created an intuitive app with AI-powered coaching, customisable meal plans, workout routines, and habit tracking features.

Design Process

Research & Discovery

  • Conducted interviews with 20 potential users
  • Analysed competitor apps
  • Created user personas
  • Defined key features

Design & Implementation

  • Created low and high-fidelity wireframes
  • Developed interactive prototypes
  • Conducted usability testing
  • Refined UI based on feedback

Key Features

AI Coach

Personalised recommendations and real-time feedback

Meal Planning

Customisable meal plans and nutrition tracking

Workout Library

Video-guided exercises and progress tracking

Key Design Outcomes

Validated Design Concepts

User testing provided overwhelmingly positive feedback on the intuitive interface and habit-building features.

Promoting Sustained Engagement

Designed a compelling user journey and rewarding experience intended to foster long-term habit adherence.

Comprehensive Design Solution

Developed a robust and scalable UI/UX framework, ready for future development and expansion.
