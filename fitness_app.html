Personal UX Case Study

FitTrack: Fitness App Redesign

A comprehensive redesign concept for a mobile application focused on tracking workouts and nutrition.

Timeline

3 months

Jan - Mar 2025

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Product Strategy

Deliverables

UX Research

UI Design System

Interactive Prototype

The Challenge

The existing fitness app had low user engagement and retention rates. Users found it difficult to track their workouts and maintain consistent nutrition logging. The challenge was to redesign the experience to make it more intuitive and motivating for users, fostering better adherence to fitness goals.

Design Process

User Research

  • Conducted 15 user interviews
  • Created user personas
  • Analysed competitor apps
  • Identified pain points

Design Solutions

  • Simplified workout tracking flows
  • Integrated gamification elements
  • Enhanced progress visualisation
  • Designed social sharing features

Key Design Outcomes

Enhanced User Engagement

Designed a more intuitive and motivating interface, addressing core pain points that led to low engagement.

Improved Usability & Experience

Streamlined complex tracking flows and introduced clear visual feedback to enhance the overall user experience.

Effective Goal Tracking

Implemented clear progress visualisation and gamification elements to better support users in achieving their fitness goals.
