Casa Artigiana: Italian Artisan Home Decor

A personal UX design project exploring an e-commerce platform celebrating Italian craftsmanship and heritage.

Role

  • Lead UI/UX Designer
  • User Research
  • Visual Design

Timeline

  • 3 months
  • 2025 Q1

Tools

  • Figma
  • Protopie
  • Miro

The Challenge 💡

My design challenge was to create an immersive e-commerce experience that showcases the **craftsmanship of Italian artisans** while making their unique products feel accessible to a global audience. The platform needed to tell compelling stories about the artisans and their regions, all while maintaining a luxurious and intuitive shopping experience.

75%

User feedback indicated desire for artisan stories

89%

User feedback highlighted value of product authenticity

The Solution

Immersive Product Stories

Each product page was designed to feature rich storytelling elements about the artisan, their craft, and regional influence to drive emotional connection and trust.

Artisan Profiles

Dedicated spaces were designed for artisans to showcase their workshop, process, and heritage, validating authenticity and building strong customer connections.

Impact & Learnings 🚀

Engaging User Experience

Successfully created an immersive and visually rich platform that effectively tells artisan stories.

Intuitive Navigation

Designed seamless user flows and a clean interface for an effortless browsing and shopping experience.

Brand Storytelling

Effectively balanced luxury aesthetics with practical e-commerce functionality to highlight product authenticity.
